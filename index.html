Algorithm Identification

Interpretability in machine learning is a growing field that we believe may be instrumental to most future AGI alignment scenarios. "Algorithm Identification" is an early-stage line of research we are proposing and evaluating which is focused on identifying algorithms present across an episode in static neural networks equipped with memory.
